About Me

 Attorney Jwayyed Jwayyed is a lifelong Ohio native. Jwayyed was born in Cincinnati, Ohio. He later moved and grew up in Akron, Ohio. Upon graduating with his bachelor’s in political science from the University of Akron, Jwayyed moved back down I-71 where he attended Capital University Law School in Columbus, Ohio. While at Capital University Law school, he obtained his Juris Doctorate with a concentration in Alternative Dispute Resolution. Since starting law school, Jwayyed has worked in various roles at general practice firms, for an insurance company, the Franklin County Public Defender’s Office, and as a municipal court prosecutor for various jurisdictions.
