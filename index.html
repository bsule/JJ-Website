About Me

Attorney Jwayyed Jwayyed is a lifelong Ohio native. Jwayyed was born in Cincinatti, Ohio. He later moved and grew up in Akron, Ohio. Upon graduating with his bachelor’s in political science from the University of Akron, Jwayyed moved back down I-71 where he attended Capital University Law School in Columbus, Ohio. While in law school, Jwayyed spent several summers at a general practice firm in Akron, Ohio. He later worked in insurance law at Safe Auto’s Cooperate Office in Easton, Ohio and then later worked for the Franklin County Public Defender’s Office. While at Capital University Law school, he obtained his Juris Doctorate with a concentration in Alternative Dispute Resolution. Attorney Jwayyed started his career as a prosecutor where he spent his first few years of practice as a municipal court prosecutor for various jurisdictions before founding Jwayyed Law LLC.

When he is not working, you will find Jwayyed at many local parks and gyms playing pick-up basketball. He enjoys being a fan of college and professional sports. Jwayyed also enjoys listening to podcasts and exploring the Metro Parks around Franklin County.
